# Emergency Standard Operating Procedures

In case of fire alarm, evacuate via the nearest marked exit, do not use elevators, and
proceed to the designated assembly point in the outer perimeter car park. For medical
emergencies, alert the nearest steward or dial the on-site medical line posted at every
gate. For lost children, report immediately to the nearest staff point; all staff points
are connected to the lost-child coordination desk. In case of severe weather, follow
public address instructions and move to covered concourse areas away from open seating.
