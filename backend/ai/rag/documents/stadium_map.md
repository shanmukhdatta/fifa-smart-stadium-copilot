# Stadium Map Knowledge

The stadium has four main gates: Gate 1 (East, near Lot A), Gate 2 (West, near Lot B),
Gate 3 (North Concourse, accessible entrance with ramps), and Gate 4 (South Concourse).
Restrooms are located behind each gate and on every concourse level. Food stalls are
concentrated on the North and South Concourses. The accessible seating sections are on
the lower bowl, sections 104-108, reachable via Gate 3 elevators.
